About Me

Hello! I’m Anushree J, an avid engineering student driven by innovation and a passionate artist exploring various crafts. In the realm of engineering, I thrive on challenges, constantly seeking innovative solutions beyond the conventional. Art is my sanctuary, and I express myself through diverse crafts. These creative endeavours provide a balance to the structured world of engineering, allowing me to explore different dimensions of my imagination. My interests extend beyond the realm of equations into the vibrant world of art and crafts. I find joy in a diverse array of creative pursuits, including quilling, watercolor painting, miniature making, crochet, Mandala art, Mehandi, and more. Each artistic endeavor becomes a unique expression, allowing me to seamlessly merge the precision of engineering with the boundless possibilities of creative exploration.
